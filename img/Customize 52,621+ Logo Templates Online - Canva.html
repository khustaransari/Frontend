Logos by Canva

Crafting a unique brand identity starts with a great logo. With Canva, logo-making is easier and more fun. Pick from our vast ready-made logo template collection or create your own. Choose from diverse logo styles—vintage, modern, or minimalist—that best communicate what you are as a brand. Then pick a logo type, either a symbol, icon, wordmark, lettermark, emblem, or a combination, that best connects with your ideal customers and clients. Increase brand recognition by adding a color palette, typography, shapes, and style layouts. Each logo design is optimized at 500 px × 500 px for the highest quality when resizing and printing.
